ABOUT OSIP

One Stop Investment Platform (OSIP) is the data/information gateway to investing in renewable energy and energy efficiency in Nigeria.

Why OSIP?

Nigeria currently has an installed electricity capacity that is far below national demand, renewable energy potential that is among the largest on the continent, and a policy environment increasingly aligned with the energy transition.

The conditions for transformative clean energy investment exist. What has been missing is a single, reliable entry point for investors who want to act and take advantage of the opportunity.

The One-Stop Investment Platform (OSIP) was built to fill that gap.

THE MANDATE

Nigeria's Official Gateway for Institutional Energy Assets

The One-Stop Energy Investment Portal (OSIP) is a presidential initiative designed to streamline the investment process in Nigeria's burgeoning energy sector. By consolidating data, regulatory approvals, and project financing tools, we eliminate bottlenecks and foster transparent growth. Our mission is to catalyze over $20B in sustainable energy investments by 2030, ensuring energy security and industrial prosperity for the nation.

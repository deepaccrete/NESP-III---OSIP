Work Package 2 — One Stop Investment Portal

Accelerating Investment in Nigeria's Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Future

The One Stop Investment Portal (OSIP) is NESP's flagship initiative — built in collaboration with its partners to give investors a single, authoritative window into Nigeria's energy market.

Explore Opportunities Read the Regulations
The Mandate

A single window for Renewable Energy & Energy Efficiency capital.

NESP, in collaboration with its partners, is designing and developing OSIP to accelerate investment in Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency. The platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools and information for making informed investment decisions.

It serves a diverse user base — local and international investors, novice and experienced stakeholders — through market intelligence, investment tools, and a user-friendly experience. With OSIP, NESP communicates Nigeria's energy investment potential directly to those positioned to act on it.

What OSIP Provides

Sector Overview

Powering Nigeria's Renewable Future

Renewable generation sits at the heart of Nigeria's 30-30-30 Vision delivering 30 GW of installed capacity with at least 30% from clean sources by 2030. Backed by the Electricity Act 2023, the sector is now open to private capital across utility-scale generation, mini-grids, and distributed solar.

From the solar-rich northern states to hydro potential in the central belt, investors can access bankable projects supported by transparent licensing and competitive fiscal incentives.

  • check_circle 100% foreign ownership permitted under the NIPC Act.
  • check_circle Pioneer Status tax holidays for qualifying projects.
  • check_circle Streamlined NERC licensing for generation & mini-grids.

Note on Methodology: OSIP data is aggregated from the Federal Ministry of Power, the Nigerian Electricity Regulatory Commission (NERC), the Rural Electrification Agency (REA), NESP partners, and verified private-sector disclosures. Investment and capacity metrics refresh every 24 hours and are reconciled quarterly against published reports. Where OSIP covers the regulatory and financial picture, the Nigeria SE4ALL platform carries the matching techno-economic project pipeline.

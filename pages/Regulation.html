Regulatory FAQ

How long does a typical licensing process take?expand_more

Standard NERC generation license applications typically take 4-6 months from the submission of a complete dossier, including the public hearing phase.

Are there restrictions on foreign equity ownership?expand_more

Under the NIPC Act, 100% foreign ownership is permitted in most energy sectors, provided the company is duly registered with the CAC.

What are the requirements for local content compliance?expand_more

Nigeria mandates specific levels of local workforce and technical service procurement under the NOGICD Act (for O&G) and power sector guidelines.
